About

Many worlds.
One voice.

"I exist in the space between stories — the ones told on screen, on the page, in the mirror, and in the silence after a hard season."

I am a storyteller with a deep interest in film, books, history, and art, drawn to exploring the emotional and cultural threads that shape how we live and relate to one another. I have worked as a production assistant on the paranormal feature film 2 Asunder, which premiered in 2024 on Phil TV. As part of the production process, I supported the development of the original look book and assisted in executing the second, including coordinating wardrobe collaborations with Miny Baruch Fashions. I also have experience in fashion styling, having collaborated with Oluwa Femmi and begun my journey as a stylist during my time on campus. Beyond film and fashion, I am the co-founder of Healing Hearts Organization, where I coordinate mental health initiatives for girls and young people navigating the lasting impact of sexual violence and childhood trauma. Through this work, Our organization has partnered with organizations such as Girls First Finance and KAPC, and contributed to community efforts including volunteering with The Nest. I am currently building Ajando, a platform where I share stories across writing, culture, and lived experience, with a focus on mental health, feminism, and the weight of being human. As I continue to grow, I am expanding my work into authorship, film production, and performance, exploring storytelling in all its forms, from the written word to the screen to embodied expression. At the core of my work is a belief that stories, whether told through film, fashion, or literature, can deepen empathy and transform how we see ourselves and each other.
